Apart from the Landseer and Hamerton prints, the collection is mainly composed of prints of local 19th century dignitaries and buildings, part of the Old Burnley Room contents, displayed for over 50 years from 1906. A few of the prints, particularly portraits of the Towneley family, are still regularly seen but otherwise the prints are seldom displayed and many are in poor condition. Those displayed here are topographical views together with four prints by David Wild made between 1980 and 1992.
