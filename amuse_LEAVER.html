Noel H. Leaver watercolours and prints

There are 33 watercolours and one pencil drawing by Noel H Leaver in the permanent collection, the earliest two being purchased from the artist in 1920. There are also 61 Leaver watercolours on loan from the Towneley Hall Society, received as a bequest from Dr Peter Bracewell in 2007.A number of prints using Leaver's paintings have been published such as a series depicting English cathedrals for annual calenders. The Towneley print collection includes six examples and there is also one print in the Bracewell collection.
