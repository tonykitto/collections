Towneley from A to Z

Silver

Summary: over 280 items, 50% being a collection of silver spoons dating from around 1890 to 1930, 50% being a mixed group mostly of sports trophies and regimental silver.

The basic details for all the objects are recorded in the Modes database with a Object Identity template name of amuse_SILVER.
