David Wild

David Wild (British painter, 1931-2014) is represented in the collections by seven oil paintings, three watercolours and four prints. He is represented at six other venues recorded by Art UK .

David was educated at Burnley Grammar School and remained in the town where he had a studio in Bridge Street. He trained at London's Slade School of Fine Art in London before winning the 1955 Abbey Scholarship in Painting at the British School in Rome. He exhibited at the Royal Academy's summer exhibition in 1973.
