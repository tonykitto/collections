Crabtree, Joseph E. (active 1941-1954)

Joseph Crabtree was a local artist represented by three oil paintings and one pencil drawing in the collections. He was President of the Burnley Artists' Society and his work was included in a local artists' exhibition at Towneley in 1953 and in Burnley Central Library in 1954.
