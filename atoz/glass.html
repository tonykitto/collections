Towneley from A to Z

Glass

Summary: around 200 items, of which around half are English 18th century drinking glasses collected by Dr Henry Holroyd in the 1920s, apart from a few drinking glasses added in the 1980s and four chandeliers, the remainder, mostly 20th Century glass, are of little artistic value but have interest such as the Queen's coronation or displaying the Borough's coat of arms.

The basic details for all the objects are recorded in the Modes database with a Object Identity template name of amuse_GLASS.
