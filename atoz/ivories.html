Towneley from A to Z

Ivories

Summary: over 200 items, most of which where collected by George Eastwood before 1903 and bequeathed to Towneley in 1906. They include carvings from Africa, China, Europe, Indian and Japan All the objects are believed to have been worked before 1947. In recent years part of the Eastwood Collection has been displayed in the Collectors Room.

The basic details for all the objects are recorded in the Modes database with a Object Identity template name of amuse_IVORY.
