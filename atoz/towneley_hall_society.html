Towneley Hall Society

The Towneley Hall Society was formed in 1965 to help stimulate interest in the hall and to protect, preserve and enhance the amenities of the hall and gardens.

It is a Registered Charity (No 515939) and raises funds for the benefit of the hall and gardens. Details of how the funds are spent can be foun on their web-site. They own the Bracewell collection of Noel H Leaver watercolours, which are on loan at Towneley.
