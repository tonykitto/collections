Towneley from A to Z

Books

There is a small collection of around 23 items, mainly 19th century relating to local history and history of the Towneley family. The library contains around 3,000 books, which are catalogued but are not part of the permanent collection.

The collection is recorded in the Modes database with a Object Identity template name of amuse_BOOKS.
