The art critic P G Hamerton created a set of prints, pencil drawings and watercolours which he gave to Burnley Mechanics Institute in 1893 for the benefit of art students. Later they went to Burnley Library before being transfered to Towneley in 1986. In total there are 135 prints with Hamerton's descriptive notes together with three coloured drawings.
